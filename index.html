Suggestions



Have some suggestions? Let me know!

Redhawk Coffee


Rehawk is located right on Meyran Avenue at the University. It also has another location which is in downtown Pittsburgh. The downtown coffee branch has a bit more seating than the Oakland one.

Contact Information: 120 Meyran Avenue, Pittsburgh PA 15213
Phone number: (412)-708-4270

Coffee Tree Roasters


Coffee Tree Roasters is located in multiple locations throughout Pittsburgh! The closest one to the University of Pittsburgh is in Shadyside. My personal favorite drink of theirs is a chai tea latte. In terms of studying, this place has a lot of seating which is surprising for coffee shops.

Contact Information: 5524 Walnut Street, Pittsburgh PA 15232
Phone number: (412)-621-6880

Big Dog Coffee


Here's a local review:
"As much as I love Big Dog coffee, I must admit that they have priced me out of going there. I cannot, in good conscience, purchase a $6+ latte anymore. It breaks my heart because the atmosphere there is very homey and inviting. There are a lot of other wonderful, reasonably-priced local coffee shops that I will go to instead. One of my favorite coffee shops in Pittsburgh. The coffee is excellent, and they have local baked goods. This is where I go to recharge - such a great atmosphere. The inside has a fireplace, a few comfy chairs, angs local artwork. There is a quaint patio with outdoor seating." - Renee Shissler

Contact Information: 2717 Sarah St, Pittsburgh, PA 15203
Phone number: (412)-586-7306

Constellation Coffee


Constellation Coffee is located in Lawrenceville. Known for its minimalist style, it has local desserts and the coffee is delicious! Plus you can walk around Lawrencville after grabbing some coffee.

Contact Information: 4059 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224
Phone number: (814) 419-9775

Crazy Mocha


Crazy Mocha also has a lot of locations around Pittsburgh. Luckily, there's one right off of Forbes Avenue. They also have a lot of different desserts. I really like their matcha green tea latte.

Contact Information: 207 Oakland Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA 15213
Phone number: (412) 621-7440

Espresso A Mano


Espresso A Mano is located in Lawrencville. The Google reviews indicate that the coffe is $. Meaning that the price isn't too high. Reviews indicate that the espresso is very good.

Contact Information: 3623 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201
Phone number: (412) 918-1864

Delanie's Coffee


Delanie's Coffee is located in Southside. I actually stumbled upon Delanie's Coffeee by accident with a friend. While I did not have coffee, I did have some of their food. They have very healthy options.

Contact Information: 1737 E Carson St, Pittsburgh, PA 15203
Phone number: (412) 927-4030

4121 Main


4121 Main has quite a creative name as it is located on 4121 Main Street in Pittsburgh. The coffee shops also sells floral plants, and in the fall a fig could be put in your drink.

Contact Information: 4121 Main St, Pittsburgh, PA 15224
Phone number: (412) 708-4500

Commonplace Coffee


Commonplace Coffee has multiple locations in Pittsburgh. The one I like to go to is in Squirrel Hill. My favorite part about the coffee is that they have different flavors such as honey, vanilla, ginger, etc.

Contact Information: 5827 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217
Phone number: (412)-422-0404

Allegheny Coffee


Allegheny Coffee has a fantastic mint pepermint coffee. Unfortunately, I think it is only available during the winter season. However, it is located in the Strip District, so there's a lot to see, eat, and try.

Contact Information: 2005 Penn Avenue, Pittsburgh PA 15222
Phone number: (412)-471-5557

Adda Coffee


I don't remember the first time I came across Adda Coffee, but since then, I have to say that it is one of my favorite coffee places to go ever. They have a delicious beet root latte, in addition to a variety of other food and drink options.

Contact Information: 200 S Highland Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206
Phone number: (412)-404-8403

Arriviste


Reviews says the coffee is a bit pricey. However, some reviewers say the coffee is the best in the neighborhood. One person said it is good for coffee snobs/lovers.

Contact Information: 5730 Ellsworth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15232
Phone number: (412)-204-7320

21st Street Coffee


The Google reviews have $$ which inicates that the coffee is a bit pricey. There's a selection of breakfast foods at this coffee shop as well. It has an "industrial-chic setting"

Contact Information: 225 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15222
Phone number: (412)-281-0121

Gasoline Street Coffee


The staff is very friendly and appears to be cheap. The Google review rates the place 4.8 out of 5 stars. The coffee is great, and there's a nice patio.

Contact Information: 643 First Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA 15219
Phone number: (412)-248-3835

Rock'n'Joe Coffee


The coffee shop is themed as Rock 'n' roll. It has a specialty food menu with drinks and sandwiches and salads.

Contact Information: 524 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15222
Phone number: (412)-281-7111
